Meet The Crown

It began with a serendipitous encounter in 2015. Greg Meredith, guitarist/composer, was making a quick stop at a mailing center when he heard singing from the back room – a voice so exquisite and effortless “it seemed to spiral through the air in dazzling arabesques”.
Tracking the song to its source, he met Natasha Natalevna and introduced himself. The following week she joined him for an informal rehearsal. Her voice, combined with Greg’s guitar stylings was pure alchemy. He instantly knew this would be the vocal “jewel in the crown” of a music project that had been brewing in his mind for the past couple of years.

From there it quickly came together, with Greg selectively gathering an eclectic ensemble of musicians to complete his artistic vision, and The Crown was launched. The band’s music is a texturally complex cultural blend - evoking elements of world, electronic, and sacred music. With a duality of precision and improvisation, it weaves and overlaps to create a distinctive musical experience.
